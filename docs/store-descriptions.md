- 📡 Indicatore LIVE con il nome dello streamer attivo
- 👥 Numero di ascoltatori connessi

- 📻 Selezione rapida delle stazioni disponibili
- 🌙 Tema scuro elegante

Progettata per essere leggera e sempre pronta — nessuna registrazione, nessun account richiesto.

## 🇬🇧 English

### Short description
Listen to Radio E45 live — music, track info and artwork in real time. Simple, elegant, always up to date.

### Full description

**Radio E45** is the official app to listen to our webradio live, directly from your Windows PC.

Open the app, hit Play and enjoy high-quality streaming. Every 10 seconds the app updates automatically, showing the current track, artist, album artwork and streamer name — so you always know what's on air.

**What's inside:**
- Live streaming with background playback
- Current track, artist and album artwork in real time
- LIVE indicator with the active streamer's name
- Number of connected listeners
- Built-in volume control
- Quick station selection
- Elegant dark theme

Designed to be lightweight and always ready — no sign-up, no account required.
